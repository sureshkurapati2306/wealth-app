How should I invest based on my risk profile?

Asset Class Breakdown

Maximise returns with your risk profile’s asset class breakdown

Your portfolio is made up of asset classes. Each asset class is a grouping of investments that has its own behaviours, risks and returns. They react differently to changing markets and economic conditions.

Different risk profiles have different recommended asset class breakdowns (weightages) built to achieve their investment goals. Follow your risk profile suggestion to get returns that suit you!

Diversification

Diversify to spread risk

Ever heard, “don’t put all your eggs in one basket?”

Invest in a mix of low risk, low return and high risk, high return asset classes. This will optimise your profits and minimise the potential of losing value if markets and economies fluctuate.

Asset Classes Asset classes are categories for investments. The investments within them have similar behaviours, risk and returns.

Low Risk, Low Return classes

These provide low steady income and are unlikely to lose value.

Fixed Income

Funds investing predominantly in bonds and similar fixed interest or fixed income securities.

Cash

The asset class for cash includes physical currency, the balances of savings and current accounts, cash ISAs, premium bonds, and money market funds.

Higher Risk, Higher Return classes

These are more likely to have bigger fluctuations in value but they also have potential for bigger gains

Local Equity

Funds investing primarily in Malaysian companies.

Regional Equity

Funds investing primarily in companies that are based in Asia Pacific countries, excluding Japan.

Global Equity

A global equity fund typically invests in foreign companies outside of Malaysia and Asia Pacific.

Portfolio Diversifier

These are more likely to have bigger enhance return opportunities, diversification or some measure of inflation protection.

Alternative

Alternative investment assets invested in non-fixed income, non-equity asset classes such as commodities, real estate, derivatives, venture capital and private equity.
