ASNB Service Hours and Cut Off Time

ASNB Service Hours:
  1. Non-Transactional Services :

    Available 24 hours

    Non-transactional services are non-monetary services such as account linking maintenance, favourite account maintenance and balance enquiry are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

  2. Transactional Services:

    02:00AM - 09:00PM Daily

Transactional Services Cut Off Time :
  1. Fixed Price Fund :

    2:00AM - 9:00PM Daily

  2. Variable Historical Price Fund

    Any transaction after 4:00pm will be processed on the next business day.

  3. Variable Forward Price Fund (Local/ Foreign)

    This is a forward pricing fund in which the fund has foreign exposure. The number of units received is determined by the price per unit of respective funds which will be available within 2 business days (transactions made before 04:00PM) or up to 3 business days (transactions made after 04:00 PM).
